Privacy Policy

ST. JUDE'S SCHOOL is committed to protecting the privacy of students, parents, staff, and website visitors. This policy outlines how personal information is collected, used, and safeguarded.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Names, contact details, date of birth, and other information provided during admission, registration, or communication.
  • Academic Information: Grades, attendance, performance records, and other educational data.
  • Online Activity: Data collected through our website forms, cookies, and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide educational services and support student learning.
  • To communicate important school updates, events, and announcements.
  • To improve school services, website functionality, and user experience.
  • To comply with legal requirements and maintain safety on campus.

3. Sharing of Information

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties.
  • Information may be shared with authorized educational or government institutions when required by law.
  • Third-party service providers may access data only for providing services on our behalf, under strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

We implement technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Access to sensitive information is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and analytics tools to monitor website performance, traffic patterns, and user preferences. This information is used solely to enhance the user experience.

6. User Rights

  • Individuals may request access to their personal information maintained by the school.
  • Requests for correction or deletion of personal data can be submitted to our data privacy officer.
  • Users may opt-out of marketing communications or newsletters at any time.

7. Policy Updates

ST. JUDE'S SCHOOL may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Users are encouraged to review this page regularly for the latest information.

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact our Data Privacy Officer at privacy@stjudesschool.edu
