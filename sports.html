Sports Facilities Overview

ST. JUDE'S SCHOOL provides a comprehensive sports program to encourage physical fitness, teamwork, and discipline. Our sports facilities are designed for both competitive and recreational activities, catering to students of all ages.

Football & Soccer

Our well-maintained football fields allow students to practice and compete at local and inter-school tournaments. Coaches provide professional training focusing on skill development and sportsmanship.

Basketball & Volleyball

Indoor and outdoor courts are available for basketball and volleyball practice. Teams are formed to participate in school leagues and regional competitions.

Track & Field

The athletics track and field area supports running, jumping, and throwing events. Students are trained in sprints, long-distance running, relays, and athletic fitness routines.

Cricket

The school has a spacious cricket ground with proper pitches and practice nets. Students are trained in batting, bowling, fielding, and game strategies. Regular matches and tournaments are organized to develop teamwork and competitive spirit.

Sports Guidelines & Safety

  • All students must wear proper sports attire and footwear.
  • Safety equipment must be used where required (helmets, guards, etc.).
  • Follow coach instructions during training and games.
  • Respect all participants and maintain fair play.
