A Message from the Principal

Committed to character, scholarship, and community.

“Education is not preparation for life; education is life itself.”

Welcome to St. Jude's School. Our mission is to nurture disciplined, curious, and responsible young minds. We uphold a learning culture grounded in tradition while adapting to the needs of the modern world.

Our academic roadmap emphasizes strong fundamentals and character formation. We believe success is built on consistent effort and values that stand the test of time.

To our students—show up, stay curious, and do the work. Excellence isn’t a mystery.

Fr. Ramesh Kujur
Principal, St. Jude’s School
