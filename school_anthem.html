School Anthem

-St.Jude's School

Common Stanza:



Enter to learn, leave to serve
This motto we hold high
With knowledge as our guiding light
We reach for the sky.


Note: Repeat it after every Stanza

Stanza 1:


Saint Jude’s students we are from the city of Lucknow.
Patron of our lives we honor you and vow
To remain loyal, steadfast and true
Guided by your light in all we pursue.

Stanza 2:


St. Jude our patron we honor and pray
Bless us and guide us day by day
In this school we find our way
Shaping hearts and minds to pave the nation’s way.

Stanza 3:


Our school moulds our life so bright
It shapes our minds and hearts alight
For service to the nation we stand so proud
With Alma Mater’s love
Our hearts are endowed.

Stanza 4:


Striving for excellence in all we do
With true knowledge our dreams come through
Like candlelight a path is bright
Every day we climb to a greater height.

Stanza 5:


Inspiring us to live the dream
St. Jude is the light that beams
For it's the reason we stand today
Grateful for the journey along the way.
