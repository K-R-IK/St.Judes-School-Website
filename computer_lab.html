Computer Lab Overview

ST. JUDE'S SCHOOL is equipped with a modern Computer Lab designed to develop digital literacy and technical skills among students. Our lab fosters practical learning through interactive sessions and advanced computing tools.

Lab Equipment

The lab houses high-performance desktop computers, projectors, printers, and interactive whiteboards. All systems are regularly updated with the latest educational software and programming tools.

Learning Programs

Students are trained in coding, digital design, simulations, and IT fundamentals. Age-appropriate software and guided projects help develop problem-solving and critical thinking skills.

Internet & Network

The lab is connected with high-speed internet and secure school network, allowing research, collaborative learning, and online assessments in a safe environment.

Lab Gallery

Lab Rules & Safety

  • Students must handle all equipment carefully.
  • No food or drinks allowed inside the lab.
  • Internet usage is monitored and restricted to educational purposes.
  • Teachers or lab assistants must be present during lab sessions.
