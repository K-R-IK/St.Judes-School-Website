Parent Resources

A streamlined hub designed to empower parents with timely, accurate, and easy-to-navigate information. This page consolidates key policies, communication channels, academic support, and essential forms.

Communication Channels

Parent–Teacher Communication

  • Monthly parent–teacher meetings scheduled as per academic calendar.
  • Dedicated email system for academic queries.
  • Teachers respond within 24–48 working hours.

Emergency & Urgent Contact

  • School office hours: 7:30 AM – 1:20 PM.
  • Emergency number available for critical situations.
  • Health updates shared promptly through SMS alerts.

Student Learning Support

Homework & Study Guides

  • Daily homework is given to the student in diary.
  • Revision worksheets available for major subjects.
  • Parents advised to monitor notebooks for personal reminders.

Important Policies

Attendance Policy

  • Minimum 75% attendance required to appear for annual exams.
  • Medical leave must be supported with documentation.
  • Repeated absenteeism leads to parental counselling.

Discipline Policy

  • Zero tolerance for bullying, teasing, or discriminatory conduct.
  • Mobile phones strictly prohibited for students.
  • Parents must reinforce school discipline norms at home.

Downloadable Forms & Circulars

  • Leave Application Form
  • Educational Trip Consent Form
  • Student Medical Card
  • Uniform Guidelines

General Guidelines for Parents

  • Ensure your child arrives at school on time and in proper uniform.
  • Check the diary regularly for notes, homework, and reminders.
  • Avoid sending expensive items or pocket money with students.
  • Promote reading habits at home for language improvement.
  • Support participation in school events and academic programs.
