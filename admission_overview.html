Admission Overview

Our admission process is structured, transparent, and aligned with the school's commitment to academic excellence and holistic development. We welcome students who demonstrate curiosity, discipline, and a readiness to grow in a nurturing environment.

Eligibility Criteria

Admission is open to all students irrespective of caste, religion, or background. Entry-level classes follow age-based guidelines, while higher classes require academic record verification.

Age Requirements

  • Nursery: 3+ years
  • Kindergarten: 4+ years
  • Grade 1: 5+ years

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate (mandatory)
  • Previous School Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Report Card of last academic year
  • Aadhar Card of student
  • Two passport-size photographs
  • Parent/Guardian ID Proof

Admission Timeline

Admissions typically open in the first week of January and continue based on the availability of seats.

Key Phases

  • Form Availability
  • Submission Window
  • Interaction/Assessment (if required)
  • Final List Announcement
  • Fee Payment & Enrollment

Why Choose Our School?

With a legacy of academic leadership, advanced facilities, and a student-first approach, we ensure every child receives the right foundation for future success.
