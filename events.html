Events & Celebrations – ST. JUDE'S SCHOOL

Events & Celebrations

Cultivating school spirit through meaningful experiences, cultural moments, and student-led initiatives.

Overview

ST. JUDE’S SCHOOL believes that true education extends beyond classrooms. Our events and celebrations offer platforms for creativity, leadership, confidence, and cultural appreciation. Every program is structured to strengthen community bonds and encourage holistic development.

Major Annual School Events

These flagship events showcase student talent and institutional excellence:

  • ✔ Annual Day Celebration – Performances, awards, cultural showcases.
  • ✔ Sports Day – Athletics, team events, marching display.
  • ✔ Founders’ Day – Honoring our heritage and values.
  • ✔ Exhibition Week – Science, art, literature and thematic displays.
  • ✔ Investiture Ceremony – Appointment of school leaders and prefects.
  • ✔ Graduation Ceremony – Farewell for passing-out batches.

Cultural & Festival Celebrations

Celebrating India’s cultural diversity is a core part of our identity. Students are encouraged to participate actively and responsibly.

  • ✔ Independence Day
  • ✔ Republic Day
  • ✔ Teacher’s Day
  • ✔ Children’s Day
  • ✔ Diwali, Christmas, Eid & other key festivals

Student-Led Initiatives

We empower students by giving them ownership of events, enhancing confidence and teamwork.

  • ✔ Club Events & Intra-school Competitions
  • ✔ Debate, Quiz & Elocution Days
  • ✔ Swachh Bharat Week & Eco Drives
  • ✔ Morning Assemblies Led by Houses

Event Planning & Management

Every event follows a structured planning process involving students, teachers, coordinators, and administration. Safety, discipline, and inclusivity remain top priorities.

  1. Event proposals are reviewed and approved by administration.
  2. Students are briefed on guidelines and responsibilities.
  3. Teachers monitor rehearsals and preparation schedules.
  4. Safety checks and emergency protocols are executed before the event.
