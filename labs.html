Science Labs Overview

At ST. JUDE'S SCHOOL, we are committed to providing our students with state-of-the-art science laboratories. Our labs are equipped with modern tools and technology to ensure hands-on learning experiences that reinforce theoretical knowledge.

Physics Lab

Our Physics Lab is designed for interactive experiments in mechanics, optics, electricity, and magnetism. Safety protocols are strictly followed to provide a secure learning environment.

Chemistry Lab

Equipped with modern apparatus, chemical reagents, and safety equipment, our Chemistry Lab allows students to perform experiments in organic, inorganic, and analytical chemistry.

Biology Lab

The Biology Lab encourages exploration of life sciences with microscopes, specimen slides, and modern biology tools. Students gain practical knowledge of anatomy, physiology, and microbiology.

Safety First

Our school prioritizes safety in all laboratory activities. Lab assistants and teachers ensure students follow strict safety measures, wear appropriate protective gear, and handle chemicals and equipment responsibly.
