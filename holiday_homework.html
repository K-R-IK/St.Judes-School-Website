Holiday Homework

To ensure students stay academically engaged while enjoying their break, the school provides thoughtfully curated holiday homework. Each assignment is designed to reinforce core concepts, promote self-learning, and keep students intellectually active during vacation.

Class-Wise Holiday Homework

Classes I to V

Students are assigned light, concept-based tasks focusing on strengthening foundational skills. Activities include:

  • Reading short stories and summarizing them
  • Basic mathematics practice worksheets
  • Drawing and creative craft work
  • Environmental observation activities
