Transport Services for Visually Impaired Students

A purpose-built mobility support system designed with dignity, safety, and accessibility.

Overview

ST. JUDE’S SCHOOL operates a dedicated and specialized transportation service exclusively for visually impaired students. The system is engineered to ensure secure, comfortable, and fully assisted mobility to and from the school campus. Every component — from bus design to staff training — is optimized to support independence without compromising safety.

Special Assistance Features

  • ✔ Trained mobility assistants available on every route.
  • ✔ Slow-boarding ramps and tactile-marked entry points.
  • ✔ Handrails with tactile grips for safe movement inside the bus.
  • ✔ Voice-based stop announcements for clear communication.
  • ✔ Mandatory seat belts designed for comfort and stability.
  • ✔ Pre-assigned seats for each student to help routine memorisation.
  • ✔ Safety equipment including first-aid kits and emergency support tools.

Safety & Support Guidelines

  1. Students receive guided boarding and deboarding by trained assistants.
  2. All buses halt completely before any movement of students.
  3. Attendants ensure every student is seated and secured before departure.
  4. Parents/guardians must hand over and collect children directly from the bus crew.
  5. No student is left unattended at stops under any circumstance.

Operational Details

The school’s transport routes are optimized to reduce travel time and ensure maximum comfort for visually impaired students. Pick-up and drop schedules are structured to avoid crowding and confusion, with personalized assistance for each child.
