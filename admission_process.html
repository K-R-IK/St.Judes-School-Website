Admission Process

Our admission workflow is streamlined to ensure a smooth and transparent experience for every parent. Follow the step-by-step guide below to complete the admission process successfully.

Step 1: Collect Admission Form

Admission forms are available at the school office during working hours. Parents may also download the form from our official website (if available).

Step 2: Submit Documents

Submit the filled admission form along with essential documents for verification.

Documents to Attach:

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous School Report Card
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhar Card
  • 2 Passport-sized Photos

Step 3: Interaction / Assessment

Students applying for higher classes may be required to attend a short assessment or interaction session with teachers to evaluate academic readiness.

Step 4: Admission Confirmation

Selected candidates will be notified through a merit list or direct communication. Parents must complete fee payment within the given timeline to confirm admission.

Step 5: Enrollment & Orientation

After confirmation, parents will receive details regarding class allotment, orientation programs, and commencement of academic sessions.

Important Guidelines

  • Incomplete forms may lead to rejection.
  • Seat availability varies by grade level.
  • Priority may be given to siblings of existing students.
  • All information provided must be accurate and verifiable.
